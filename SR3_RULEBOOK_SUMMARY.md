# SR3 Rulebook Summary

## Key Rules for Shadowrun 3rd Edition Character Creation

---

## SKILL COST RULES

### Base Skill Cost
- **1 point per level** for rating ≤ linked attribute
- **2 points per level** for rating > linked attribute

### Specialization Rules
- **When you specialize, you REDUCE the base skill by 1 and INCREASE the specialization by 1**
- Notation "2/4" means: Unarmed Combat 2 (base, reduced by 1), Kicking 4 (specialization, increased by 1)
- Cost is based on the HIGHER rating: max(baseSkill, specialization)
- Example: Unarmed Combat 3 + Kicking specialization = Unarmed Combat 2, Kicking 4 = max(2, 4) = 4 points
- Example: Unarmed Combat 3 (no specialization) = 3 points

### Specialization vs No Specialization Comparison:

| Base Rating | No Specialization | With Specialization |
|-------------|-------------------|---------------------|
| 3 | 3 points | Unarmed 2, Kicking 4 = 4 points |
| 4 | 4 points | Unarmed 3, Kicking 5 = 5 points |
| 5 | 5 points | Unarmed 4, Kicking 6 = 6 points |

## SKILL RATINGS

- **Maximum rating**: 6 (or Attribute + 2)
- **Specialization**: Can specialize in a specific use of a skill
- **Specialization notation**: "Skill Name (Specialization)" or "Skill (Rating/Attribute)"

## EXAMPLE CALCULATIONS

### Example 1: Quickness 6, Edged Weapons 5
- Rating 1-5: 5 levels × 1 point = 5 points
- Total: 5 points

### Example 2: Quickness 6, Edged Weapons 6
- Rating 1-6: 6 levels × 1 point = 6 points
- Total: 6 points

### Example 3: Quickness 5, Edged Weapons 6
- Rating 1-5: 5 levels × 1 point = 5 points
- Rating 6: 1 level × 2 points = 2 points
- Total: 7 points

### Example 4: Unarmed Combat 3 + Kicking 2 (Specialization)
- Base skill: Unarmed Combat 3 = 3 points
- Specialization: Kicking 2 = included in base cost
- Total: max(3, 2) = 3 points

## PRIORITY SYSTEM

### Skill Points by Priority
- **Priority A**: 30 points
- **Priority B**: 40 points
- **Priority C**: 30 points
- **Priority D**: 24 points
- **Priority E**: 15 points

---

## CHARACTER CREATION WORKFLOW

1. **Choose Metatype** (Priority)
2. **Choose Attributes** (Priority) - Apply racial modifiers
3. **Choose Magic/Resonance** (Priority)
4. **Choose Skills** (Priority) - Allocate skill points
5. **Choose Resources** (Priority) - Starting nuyen
6. **Purchase Gear** - Weapons, cyberware, etc.
7. **Purchase Contacts**
8. **Finalize** - Calculate derived stats

---

## NOTES

- Specializations do NOT cost extra skill points
- Always use the HIGHER of base skill rating or specialization rating
- Skill cost doubles only for levels ABOVE the linked attribute
- Knowledge skills follow the same cost rules as active skills
